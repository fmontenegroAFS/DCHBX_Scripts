The way that you file your taxes affects your eligibility for certain medical insurance programs. You must indicate all Application Group members who are expected to file taxes this year. Most adults who receive any sort of income are expected to file taxes.

For married couples filing together, please choose both filers. Please do not select individuals who will be listed as dependents on your tax return, unless they are also required to file their own tax return.

The amount of income that requires an individual to file taxes depends on the person's filing status and age. Those under age 65 with income over the following amounts are required to file taxes:

  1. Single - $10000; Married filing jointly - $20000; Head of household - $12850; Tax Dependent - Earned Income over $5950 or Unearned Income over $950.

These amounts are slightly higher for tax households including members who are over 65 and/or blind.
