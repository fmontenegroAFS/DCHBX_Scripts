Please Tell Us About Your Application Group
What is this Section About
In this section, you will be asked questions about the rest of your Application Group, starting with whether there are any other members of your Application Group and then details about each of them if there are. Once all the Application Group members have been entered, you will be asked questions about the relationships between each of the people in your Application Group and also about how you expect to file taxes in the coming year. This information is important in order to be sure that everyone in your Application Group is getting the assistance with their health care that they are entitled to.
For this section you may need
The details of anyone else in your Application Group
Their SSNs
Their relationships to you and each other
