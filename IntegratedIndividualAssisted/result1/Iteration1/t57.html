Please Tell Us About Your Application Group Income
What is this Section About
In this section you will be asked to provide information on the income of each of the people in your application group. We may need to know about both your current income and what you expect your income to be for the coming year. We will access other state and federal data systems and use any information to determine your eligibility.
For this section you may need
The last tax return made by anyone in your Application Group
The latest wage information for anyone in your Application Group
Details of any other income received by anyone in your Application Group
