Are you a District of Columbia resident?

A resident of DC is someone who currently is living in DC, not for a temporary purpose or who has an intent to leave DC. This can also include someone who temporarily resides outside DC but who plans to return, who has come to DC to find employment, or who has a job commitment in DC.
