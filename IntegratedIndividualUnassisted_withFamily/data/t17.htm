Individual and Family Health Insurance

Signing up for health insurance is an easy threefour-step process

Step 1
Can you get coverage now?

See if you qualify for health insurance now. Apply anytime for Medicaid or the Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP).

Step 1 2
Tell us about yourself

Who needs coverage? You? You and your family? After you tell us, find out if you qualify for tax credit savings in less than 5 minutes.

Step 2 3
Compare plans and choose

Find a quality, affordable healthcare plan that's right for you, or for you and your family. There are many choices to meet your needs and budget.

Step 3 4
Enroll

Once you choose a plan, quickly create an account and enroll. If you qualify for tax credit savings, create an account and complete your application.
